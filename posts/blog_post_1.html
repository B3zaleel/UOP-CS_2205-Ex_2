Books every Developer should Read

To become better at developing software that would be loved by your customers, you need to know how your customer works, and these list of books would help you do just that.

By Nagato Uzumaki | April 14, 2024

Share on:

1. Code Complete


Code Complete 2 is yet another masterpiece from Steve McConnell for software developers that really love what they do and want to get better at it. Not a lot of programmers read technical books, so reading this one would give you a step up.

2. The Mythical Man-Month


This is one of those books that I really believe a lot of programmers should have read. This book would show you how different computers are from people aside one being a machine and not the other. I guarantee that this book is certainly going to be worth your time.
